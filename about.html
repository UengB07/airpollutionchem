ABOUT

This site was made by Benjamin Ueng. Why was it made? I thought it would just be a good idea to throw some chemistry or air pollution fundamentals around for any people wanting to learn some basic science behind the phenomena. Of course, this does not go too much into detail and other experts will know more. Still, this site could be useful.
